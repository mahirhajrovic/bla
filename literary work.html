    • Literary work

    Besides his patents he published several dozen scientific disputes, articles, reviews and a 396-page autobiography under the name Michael Pupin, From Immigrant to Inventor (Scribner's, 1923). He won the annual Pulitzer Prize for Biography or Autobiography. It was published in Serbian in 1929 under the title From pastures to scientist (Od pašnjaka do naučenjaka).

    Beside this he also published:
    Naslov dela Der Osmotische Druch und seine Beziehung zur Freien Energie, Inaugural Dissertation zur Erlangung der Doctorwurde, Buchdruckerei von Gustav Shade izdavac Buchdruckerei von Gustav Shade mjesto Berlin godina 1889
    Naslov dela Thermodynamics of Reversible Cycles in Gases and Saturated Vapors izdavac John Wiley & Sons mjesto berlin godina 1894
    Naslov dela Serbian Orthodox Church izdavac J. Murray mjesto london godina 1918
    Naslov dela Yugoslavia izdavac American Association for International Conciliation mjesto America godina 1919
    Naslov dela The New Reformation; from Physical to Spiritual Realities izdavac Scribner mjesto new york godina 1927
    Naslov dela Romance of the Machine izdavac Scribner mjesto new york godina 1930
    Naslov dela Toward Civilization izdavac Green& Co mjesto new york godina 1930
